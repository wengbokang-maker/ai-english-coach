# 每日口语练习 Prompt 模板

## 使用说明

把以下 Prompt 复制到 ChatGPT / 豆包，直接开始练习。

---

## 📌 核心 Prompt：每日口语陪练

```
You are my English speaking coach. I want to practice conversational English for 15 minutes.

My level: intermediate (I can form basic sentences but struggle with fluency)
My goal: improve daily work and life conversational English
Today's focus: [填写今天的主题，如：talking about my work / casual small talk / describing my weekend]

Please:
1. Start with a simple warm-up question
2. Correct my grammar naturally (don't interrupt, add corrections at the end)
3. Introduce 1-2 new useful phrases during our conversation
4. Give me a summary at the end with: what I did well, what to improve, and today's key phrase

Let's begin!
```

---

## 📌 场景练习：工作汇报

```
Let's practice a work meeting scenario.
You are my colleague, and I need to give you a project update.
The project is: [填写你的项目名]
Current status: [填写进度]

Please respond naturally, ask follow-up questions, and help me express my ideas in better English.
After 10 minutes, give me feedback on my language use.
```

---

## 📌 场景练习：闲聊维持

```
Let's have a casual English conversation for 10 minutes.
Topic: [今日话题，如：weekend plans / favorite food / a recent movie]

Rules:
- Keep it natural and friendly
- If I stop or struggle, give me a hint (not the full sentence)
- Use simple vocabulary, avoid jargon
- End by teaching me one useful casual expression from our chat
```

---

## 📌 发音练习 Prompt

```
Help me practice pronunciation.
I'll say a sentence, please:
1. Identify any pronunciation issues
2. Explain how to fix them (mouth position, stress, rhythm)
3. Give me the sentence broken into syllables
4. Let me try again

My problem areas: [填写你的问题，如：th sound / r vs l / word stress]
```

---

## 📝 使用建议

- 每天选 **1-2 个 Prompt** 使用，不要贪多
- 把好的对话截图保存，作为学习日志
- 发现新的好用 Prompt？欢迎 PR 到这个文件！
